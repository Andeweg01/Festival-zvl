Boundless Classical Pearls

18 September - 16 October 2020


Festival van Zeeuwsch-Vlaanderen creates concert halls behind the dikes every year. Special, often surprising locations in this noteworthy, different area, situated in the periphery of the art cities and concert halls in Flanders. The chosen theme for edition 32 is:

Boundless Classical Pearls

With it's setting in rural peace and mixed culture the Festival van Zeeuwsch-Vlaanderen proudly settled itself among the well known classical music events in Western Europe and has a great reputation within The Netherlands and abroad. It's a fixed value for Zealand's cultural agenda. Every year you're sure to experience great symphony orchestras, chamber music ensembles, soloist who are often winners of national and international musical competitions.

Come and discover how contemporary classical music actually is. You're most welcome!
